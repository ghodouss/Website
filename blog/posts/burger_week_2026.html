Santa Barbara Burger Week Prospective Itinerary

Santa Barbara
Published

February 19, 2026

This is the set of restaurants for which I am the most personally excited about during Santa Barbara burger week. This list factors in variety, convenience, and excitement. I may update this list as I eat. I may even get crazy and visit a place not on my top picks. I will keep you posted.
